Happy birthday, Rhain.

You’re 22 now.
Maybe this is the first time I’ve made something special for your birthday.
This whole journey, this gift, was made with just “copy and paste”…
But it carries all the memories and love I feel for you.

When we first met, I was 16 and you were 18 (I think).
Thinking back on those days and everything since—
there are too many memories to fit into this one website.

So I’ve prepared another way to keep our memories together.
(You’ll find out soon.)

It’s always been hard for me to express my feelings through letters.
But I want you to know—
making this website for you has never once felt like a chore.

I started writing this letter around March 27th,
and now, as this journey ends, I want to say one thing:

Thank you for traveling with me.
From Perth to Osaka,
you’ve collected every moment with me,
and each of those memories is one of the most important in my entire life.

Every place, every laugh, every scene—
they’ve all settled deep in my heart,
and I’ll remember them forever as if they happened just yesterday.

Next time, we’ll meet in your country.
I’ll be there to see your graduation ceremony,
and I’m really looking forward to it.

I love you.
